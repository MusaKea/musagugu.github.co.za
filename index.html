Boxfusion Reseller Partner Programme — Built from the ground up

The problem I identified

Boxfusion had partners — but they existed informally, without documentation, structure, or a system to manage them. There was no way to track partner activity, no formal agreements, no onboarding process, and no commercial framework to govern how revenue would be shared.

More critically, the business needed to expand its market footprint and reach new customers it couldn't serve directly. Partners were the answer — but only if they were properly structured, enabled, and managed.

I saw this gap and took ownership of solving it.

What I built
  • Designed and documented the full Boxfusion Reseller Partner Programme from scratch
  • Created a three-tier partner structure (Bronze, Silver, Gold) with clear criteria, margin structures ranging from 10% to 35%+, and distinct benefits per tier
  • Built the onboarding process — application, assessment, welcome pack, contract finalisation, and 90-day engagement planning
  • Developed the partner portal to support programme management, tracking, and communication
  • Designed ongoing partner enablement through training access, demand generation support, and Partner Success Manager assignment
  • Briefed and structured the company intranet to centralise the systems and resources that support the programme internally
